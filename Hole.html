Schwarzschild Radius
So, what's this mean? Well, the Schwarzchild Radius (linky) is a magical 'size' for a given mass m,
below which the density of that mass is so great the the escape velocity excedes the speed of light (c)!
Below this radius, you form a singularity, or black hole.
